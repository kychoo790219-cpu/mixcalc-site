Three reasons your lab's spreadsheets keep failing audits.

After 10+ years preparing gravimetric gas mixtures, we kept seeing the same failure modes. MixCalc Pro was built to eliminate every one of them.

01

Ideal gas assumption at 150+ bar

Most in-house spreadsheets use PV=nRT and silently introduce 2–5% mass errors. MixCalc applies Peng-Robinson EOS with 78 gases in the critical database.

02

Uncertainty hand-calculations

ISO/IEC 17025 auditors require full uncertainty budgets per ISO 6142-1 Annex F/G. Most labs calculate these manually — error-prone and non-reproducible.

03

Certificate of Analysis generation

COA documents take 30+ minutes per cylinder to draft in Word. MixCalc generates auditor-ready PDFs in one click, formatted to the standards your clients expect.

Every feature your team actually uses.

No AI-generated marketing fluff. Real capabilities built for real gas mixture workflows.

Z

Peng-Robinson EOS

Full mixing-rule implementation with 78 gases in the critical properties database. Automatic fallback warnings when data is missing. Compressibility factor calculated per component and for the combined mixture.

U

ISO 6142-1 Annex F/G Uncertainty

Full u(x_i) and expanded U(x_i) calculation per the GUM framework. Input weighing uncertainty, parent gas purity uncertainty, and IUPAC 2021 molar mass uncertainty — output auditable to ISO/IEC 17025 standards.

P

Pre-mixed base cylinder mode

Match your actual production process: use a multi-component parent mixture + balance gas injection. Automatic ratio-match tolerance check warns when base composition drift exceeds ±2%.

W

Multi-step dilution wizard

Can't weigh the target directly? The wizard back-calculates the optimal intermediate gas concentration that satisfies your balance's minimum weighable mass, then generates the step-by-step preparation procedure.

C

Compatibility checks

Based on ISO 11114-1/2 principles. Catches oxidizer + fuel, acid + base, water-reactive, and cylinder material incompatibilities before they become incidents. Not a substitute for SDS — but catches 90% of common mistakes.

$

Cost estimator

Per-gas unit price entry with automatic cost calculation based on injected mass. Compare parent gas suppliers side by side. Export cost breakdown as CSV for internal review or customer quotes.
